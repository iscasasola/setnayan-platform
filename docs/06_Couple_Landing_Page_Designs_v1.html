Anchor Font
Cormorant Garamond (Google) — luxurious classical serif. Italicized at 124px for "Maria & Juan", italicized again for all section titles and pull-quotes.
Body Font
Manrope (Google) — friendly modern sans with rounded forms. Used for body, labels, schedule times, and form fields. Rounded geometry rhymes with the monogram.
Star of Show
Custom inline-SVG monogram: interlocked M & J inside a circle, with sampaguita-inspired flower above and a calligraphic flourish below. Defined once via <symbol>, reused everywhere.
Visual Rhymes
Monogram repeats at 5 scales (hero, section-eyebrow, schedule divider, RSVP button, footer, watermark). Champagne-gold rule under every heading. Circular thumbnails rhyme the monogram's curves.
Depth Treatments
Subtle dotted SVG-pattern background (1px dots / 28px grid). Soft shadows on cards (0 12px 40px aubergine/.06). Gold gradient on RSVP button. Faint text-shadow letterpress on hero serif.
Opacity Hierarchy
Couple H1 100% · Date subhead 78% · Venue 65% · Body 72% · Pullquote 95% · Watermark 35%. Gold rule at 100% but only 1px tall — punctuation, not pressure.
Tayo Design System Recommendations

Our pick: Variation C — Motif Symbol

Primary anchor font
Cormorant Garamond

Italicized headlines feel handwritten and intentional — the opposite of templated. Pairs beautifully with Filipino visual culture (calligraphy, embroidery, lace).

Primary body font
Manrope

Friendly rounded sans that scales from app UI to landing page without feeling cold. Renders well at every size and weight.

Primary brand palette
Pearl · Aubergine · Gold

Warm, ceremonial, and differentiated from the wedding-tech crowd (which leans heavy on millennial pink and sage). Champagne gold is the signature accent.

Why this combination wins for Tayo

Variation C builds in the strongest Tayo-specific advantage: every couple gets a one-of-one mark. The monogram is a system, not a template — couples generate their own glyph and it propagates across their landing page, invitations, and in-app moments. That's the kind of distinctive personality that signals "made with care, not generated" — exactly the editorial, non-AI feel the brand is reaching for. Cormorant + Manrope is also the most stylistically flexible pairing: it works for elegant Manila weddings, garden weddings in Tagaytay, and beach weddings in Siargao without re-skinning.
