## 2026-06-22 · feat(invitation): branded per-guest QR auto-shows when CUSTOM_QR_GUEST is paid

Owner-locked rule (2026-06-22): a paid SKU must auto-surface in its natural in-context place once the couple owns it — and "owns" for our manual payment rails means admin-APPROVED (`eventSkuActive`), not the pending-inclusive `eventOwnsSku`.

Applied to the paid Custom QR per Guest (`CUSTOM_QR_GUEST`) upgrade so it auto-appears on the Invitation surface the moment payment is approved, instead of staying buried in the studio detail page:

- **Auto-show on the Invitation page** (`apps/web/app/dashboard/[eventId]/invitation/page.tsx`): the SKU is resolved ONCE at the top of the page via `eventSkuActive(createAdminClient(), eventId, 'CUSTOM_QR_GUEST')` — read with the ADMIN client because ownership is an event-level fact and `orders` RLS is purchaser-scoped (a co-host would otherwise be mis-gated). When active, every guest's QR (table thumbnails, mobile list, branding preview) renders BRANDED (`renderBrandedInvitationQrSvg`, palette-tinted from the Mood Board reception→bride→ceremony→monogram color) instead of the plain `renderInvitationQrSvg`; the "Print sheet (A4)" link points at the branded print sheet (`/studio/custom-qr-guest/print`), and per-guest "Download PNG" affordances appear pointing at the branded PNG endpoint `/api/website/qr/guest/[guestId]`. When NOT active, the plain QR + plain print sheet render exactly as before — zero change for non-owners. Degrades to the plain QR on any entitlement-read error (always-rendered page must not crash).
- **Studio gate fixed** (`apps/web/app/dashboard/[eventId]/studio/custom-qr-guest/page.tsx`): the OWNED/branded-view gate switched from `eventOwnsSku` → `eventSkuActive`, so the branded cards + downloads appear only after admin payment approval — matching the branded PNG-download API and the new Invitation auto-show. The buy CTA (`InlineCheckoutDrawer` in `UnownedView`) prevents double-buy on its own, so no separate `eventOwnsSku` guard is needed.
- **Studio branded print sheet aligned** (`apps/web/app/dashboard/[eventId]/studio/custom-qr-guest/print/page.tsx`): its branded-feature gate likewise moved `eventOwnsSku` → `eventSkuActive`, so a pending-payment couple can't reach the branded print pack before approval (it was previously inconsistent with the detail page + PNG API). This is a pure feature gate, not a buy CTA.

Wired against existing renderer + route (no new ones invented): `renderBrandedInvitationQrSvg` / `resolveBrandedQrColors` from `apps/web/lib/qr.ts`, and the PNG route `apps/web/app/api/website/qr/guest/[guestId]/route.ts` (already `eventSkuActive`-gated).
