## 2026-07-05 · feat(vendors): /open-shop onboarding collects the owner-required logo + company email

Owner directive: the `/open-shop` vendor on-ramp (the "List your business for free" CTA target) must capture **exactly the six basics** — shop name, shop **logo** (mandatory per §2.1b), owner name, contact number, **company email**, and one primary service. The already-shipped onboarding wizard (owner 2026-07-03) collected shop name · primary service · location · contact name · contact number · website · social, but **omitted the logo and the company email** — the two owner-required fields this change folds in. It stays **additive**: the existing location / website / social capture and the Get-verified `social_media` slot seeding are untouched.

- **`app/open-shop/_components/open-shop-wizard.tsx`**: Step 1 now carries the mandatory **logo** upload (reusing the shared `<FileUpload bucket="media" variant="square" qrGuard>` → R2 pattern from My Shop, identical props to `vendor-dashboard/profile/page.tsx`); Step 2 renames "Contact name" → **Owner name** and adds a required **Company email** field. Owner name + contact number are now required (were optional). Closing note added: *"You can upgrade your business further anytime — add photos, services, pricing and more from your dashboard."*
- **`app/open-shop/actions.ts` `becomeVendor`**: validates + upserts `logo_url` (r2:// ref, same `parseLogoValue` shape as My Shop) and `contact_email` (light shape check) onto `vendor_profiles`, alongside the existing `business_name` / `business_owner_name` / `contact_phone` / `services`. Owner name, contact number, and company email are now hard-required with inline error redirects. Provisioning path unchanged: idempotent find-or-create of the owned `vendor_profiles` row + founding `vendor_team_members` admin seat via the admin client (a fresh signup's trigger-provisioned bare shop is UPDATEd; a logged-in account with no shop gets one INSERTed). No publish/verify here — new shops keep default visibility.
- **`app/open-shop/page.tsx`**: selects `logo_url` + `contact_email`, pre-resolves the logo display URL via `displayUrlForStoredAsset` so an already-uploaded logo paints on load, and passes `vendorProfileId` (scopes the logo R2 prefix) into the wizard. The logged-out auth gate now redirects to `/signup?as=vendor&next=/open-shop` so the return loop is explicit (covers the already-logged-in `?as=vendor` path, not just fresh signups whose vendor default already lands on `/open-shop`).

Column names written: `business_name`, `logo_url`, `business_owner_name`, `contact_phone`, `contact_email`, `services[]` (primary category leads the array). No new migration — every column already exists on `vendor_profiles`.
