The pile (what changed). Each parent category header now pins at a stepped offset (top = bar + index × 38px) so they compile on top instead of replacing each other. All 10 stacked ≈ half the frame; the recap fills the bottom half. The vendor's 5 pipeline stages pile the same way.

· Top stats bar → couple: Chosen / Range vs budget · vendor: Booked / In-talks vs dates.

· 300px card → couple: a vendor · vendor: a couple. Gold "chosen" → locked pick / booked wedding.

· ⇄ Compare appears on any category with 2+ shortlisted (Caterer now; Photographer after the ▶ demo). Opens a like-for-like sheet — price / reviews / distance / highlights, cheapest + top-rated flagged. It never changes your pick — you still lock from the card.

When the couple shortlists (the ▶ demo): couple sees the card land in their plan; vendor sees an aggregate "saved by N couples" tick — never the name/contact (RA 10173). It becomes a named "New inquiry" only when they message. Decisions to confirm: (1) is the half-screen stack the right height, or do you want it taller/shorter?
